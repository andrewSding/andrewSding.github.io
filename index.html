Black Campus Ministries
LEARN MORE
Black Campus Ministries at UC Berkeley is a movement of students seeking to give our whole lives to Jesus and to serve and love the Black student community. Through our Bible discussions and performing arts groups, we provide spaces for anyone to get to know Jesus and to be a part of a loving, welcoming family.
Cal Christian Fellowship
LEARN MORE
Cal Christian Fellowship (IVCCF) is a multi-ethnic community seeking to learn more about Jesus and how He's relevant to our lives. We meet regularly for weekly small group bible studies and large group fellowship meetings to grow deeper in our faith, stronger as a community, and wider in our impact. Our Vision: To experience, embody, and share the unconditional love of Jesus with others.
La Fe

La Fe @ Cal is a Chican@/Latin@ familia of visionaries and doers who respond to God’s word for the empowerment and transformation of our communities. This semester we’re starting some new things, new groups to gain some perspective on where God is in these areas of our lives.

Intervarsity International Student Ministry (ISM) has a really exciting vision: We want to understand and love each other, God, and the world. Intervarsity International Student Ministry (ISM) is a community of friends: we are American and international students we are Christians and people of different perspectives we are exchange students, undergrads, and graduate students.
Kapwa
LEARN MORE
Kapwa’s vision is to see Filipino-Americans at Cal love and follow God, understand the goodness and impact of their identity, and become a people who actively love themselves, their campus, and their world. Kapwa is a space for anyone interested in learning more about God’s love for his people as well as issues of faith, culture, and justice.
Greek IV
LEARN MORE
God loves Greeks! We are a community of fraternity and sorority students bringing together Greek life and faith life. We believe that it is possible to follow Jesus AND be Greek at the same time, and are influencing the Greek system from the inside out. We welcome people from all councils, spiritual backgrounds, and walks of life! Come join our movement!
